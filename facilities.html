Our Facilities and Services



At Orchard Stables we offer you our well-kept facilities for you and your horse to take full advantage of, including our standout amenity, the Collingham Horse Adventure Park. All facilities are free to use for our liveries, and are available to hire for non-liveries.

If you wish to keep your horse here with us, we offer different livery services to suit you and your horse, these range from grass livery to full livery with a stable. Our livery packages range from DIY for £30 per week to full livery for £100 per week.

A full service and rates list can be obtained on request by contacting us, where you will be invited to visit us here at Orchard Stables.

30 Person Classroom
__________

Our classroom seats approximately 30 people, and it is available to hire by the hour for equine talks and demos. Classroom hire includes tea, coffee, and biscuits for all attendees. If requested, bacon/sausage butties or a vegetarian alternative can be ordered in advance, and there is a chip shop and co-op in the village, just a short walk or drive away. The classroom is directly next to the arena and the lunge-pen if these are required at the same time. Previously we have had talks from Karl Greenwood on rider-confidence; Integravet on Chiropractic and Acupuncture treatment for animals; Donna Case BSc Hons on Equine Nutrition; Darren Unwin on Equine Iridology and many more.

The classroom can be hired from £15 per hour.

20 x 40 Outdoor Arena
__________

Our 20m x 40m arena is the perfect environment to coach your horse. The arena has been surfaced with an all-weather surface which is leveled weekly to maintain it, enabling riders to enjoy this arena nearly all year round (though we do have alternatives if extreme weather doesn't permit use). We also have a full set of show jumps which are free to use for those keen jumpers. The area is fully CCTV-monitored and can also be floodlit when the light begins to fade. There is also a sound-system if you wish to ride to music.

The arena can be hired from £10 for non-liveries.

Secure Tack Room
__________

The tack room at Orchard Stables also combines as an office and a kitchen, with a kettle, fridge, tea, coffee, and biscuits (if you get there quick enough!). It is also the area where any notices go up on the board about clinics, vet or dentist visits, or anything else there is to share. In the tack room, we have a designated space for each horse's bridle and saddle, although there is also a large, dry storage barn with separate bays for each livery to store your feed, grooming kit, rugs, hats, boots and any other horsey bits you may have; as well as another bay for your hay bales and mucking out equipment.

Post and Rail or Electric Fencing
__________

Orchard Stables offers all year round grazing, providing both winter and summer fields, which are situated on sandy soil, ensuring that the ground is bog-free. Individual fields are provided for each horse, although you can choose to have your own herd turned out together. Some fields come with automated water troughs, and for those without, large water containers are situated close to the fields for easy access. There is a choice of either post-and-rail fencing and electric, we will do our best to suit you and your horse's preference of fencing.

Large Rubber Matted Stables
__________

We have 15 large rubber-matted stables situated around an expansive concrete courtyard, with plenty of space to groom, wash or tend to your horse, a space which is also available for those visiting for clinics or camping. In our stables, we use wood pellet bedding, which is both economic and easy to muck-out. This can be bought from the stables. Some stables have automated water, although the tap is just metres away from each stable.

Collingham Horse Adventure Park
__________

Collingham Horse Adventure Park, also known as CHAPs, is the first adventure park of its kind. An adventure park for equines! The park is a place to bring your horse or pony and have some fun. Try out the roller coaster, log roll, tunnel of love, water flume, ghost train and loads more. There is an assortment of small cross country jumps and obstacles. A place for you and your horse to have a go at horse agility, trail, TREC, natural horsemanship or somewhere to just help bombproof your horse and help both of you gain confidence. Free for our liveries.

CHAPS can be hired for £15 per horse.
Or £10 per pony under 14hh.
